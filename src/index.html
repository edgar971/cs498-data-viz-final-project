An analysis of Mobile Apps (Apple iOS App Store)

There are tons of Mobile apps available to users of various categories and it's important to know what insights they provide. The dataset contains price, rating, genre, and other information about the app. This analyses 7200 available in the iOS App Store to answers the following questions:

  1. What does the overall dataset show?
  2. What categories of apps get the most reviews?
  3. Does the price impact the number of reviews and their rating?

Overview

The overview shows the app categories, the number of apps, reviews, rating average, and price average. The majority of the apps are games which also has the most number of reviews. Medical apps have the lowest number of reviews and the highest price average. Productivity apps have the highest user rating average. Feel free to explore the overview table below:
